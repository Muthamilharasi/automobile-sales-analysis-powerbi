# 🚗 Automobile Sales Analysis Dashboard (Power BI)

## 📌 Business Problem

Automobile companies generate large volumes of sales data, but without proper analysis, it becomes difficult to identify high-performing products, regional demand patterns, and growth opportunities.

This project aims to transform raw CSV sales data into actionable insights that can support strategic business decisions.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

* Evaluate overall sales performance
* Identify top-performing vehicle categories and models
* Analyze regional demand and revenue contribution
* Detect seasonal sales trends
* Highlight underperforming segments

---

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies

* Power BI (Dashboard & Visualization)
* Power Query (Data Cleaning & Transformation)
* DAX (Calculated Measures)
* CSV Dataset

---

## 🧹 Data Preparation (Power Query)

The dataset was imported in CSV format and cleaned using Power Query inside Power BI.

### Key Transformations:

* Removed duplicates and irrelevant columns
* Handled missing/null values
* Standardized inconsistent text fields
* Converted data types (date, numeric)
* Created calculated columns for analysis

---

## 📊 Dashboard Highlights

* Total Revenue & Units Sold KPIs
* Monthly & Yearly Sales Trends
* Region-wise Performance Analysis
* Product Category Breakdown
* Interactive Filters for dynamic exploration

---

## 🔍 Key Insights

* 🚘 A small set of vehicle categories contributed the majority of total revenue, indicating strong product concentration
* 📈 Sales showed clear seasonal peaks, suggesting demand cycles that can be leveraged for marketing
* 🌍 Certain regions consistently outperformed others, highlighting geographic revenue drivers
* 📉 A few product segments underperformed, indicating potential inefficiencies or low demand

---

## 💡 Business Recommendations

* 📌 Focus marketing and inventory on top-performing vehicle categories to maximize revenue
* 📌 Align promotional campaigns with seasonal demand peaks to increase sales efficiency
* 📌 Invest in high-performing regions while analyzing barriers in low-performing markets
* 📌 Re-evaluate or redesign underperforming product segments

---

## 🚀 Business Impact

This dashboard enables stakeholders to:

* Make data-driven sales and marketing decisions
* Identify growth opportunities and risks
* Optimize product and regional strategies
